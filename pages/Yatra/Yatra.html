Project Overview

Purpose

A comprehensive travel planning platform that helps users organize trips, manage budgets, and coordinate with travel companions for seamless journey planning.

Target Users

Solo travelers, families, and groups looking for an organized approach to travel planning with budget management and collaborative features.

Development Time

4 weeks of development focusing on user experience, responsive design, and interactive travel planning tools.

Key Innovation

Integrated budget calculator with real-time expense tracking and group chat functionality for collaborative trip planning.

How Yatra Works

Plan a Trip

Start by choosing your start and end dates with a memorable trip name. Set your destination and begin organizing your perfect journey.

Invite Your Friends

Easily invite friends and family to join your trip planning adventure. Collaborate together to create the perfect group experience.

Chat in Real Time

Communicate instantly with your travel companions through our integrated chat system. Share ideas, make decisions, and stay connected.

AI-Powered Recommendations

Trigger smart recommendations by using the keyword @Yatra in chat to get personalized suggestions for places, foods, and hotels in your destination.

Real-time Trip Flowchart

Visualize your complete trip plan with an interactive flowchart that displays daily activities for you and your travel companions in real-time.

Smart Budgeting System

Allocate appropriate budgets for each day of your trip with our intelligent budgeting system. Track expenses and stay within your planned budget.
