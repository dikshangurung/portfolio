Project Overview

Purpose

A comprehensive travel planning platform that helps users organize trips, manage budgets, and coordinate with travel companions for seamless journey planning.

Target Users

Solo travelers, families, and groups looking for an organized approach to travel planning with budget management and collaborative features.

Development Time

4 weeks of development focusing on user experience, responsive design, and interactive travel planning tools.

Key Innovation

Integrated budget calculator with real-time expense tracking and group chat functionality for collaborative trip planning.
