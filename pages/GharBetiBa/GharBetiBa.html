Project Overview

Purpose

A comprehensive rental marketplace platform that connects property owners with potential tenants using advanced mapping features, real-time communication, and location-based matching.

Target Users

Property owners looking to rent out their properties and tenants searching for suitable rental accommodations with location preferences and community features.

Development Time

6 weeks of development focusing on mapping integration, user experience, and real-time communication features.

Key Innovation

Interactive polygon drawing on maps for precise location preferences and geo-matching algorithms for optimal tenant-property pairing.

How GharBetiBa Works

Browse Properties

Start by exploring available rental properties in your preferred area. Use our intuitive search and filtering system to find properties that match your requirements.

Draw Your Preferred Area

Use our interactive map to draw polygon areas where you'd like to find rental properties. This helps us match you with properties in your exact preferred locations.

View Property Details

Explore detailed property information including amenities, pricing, availability calendar, and high-quality photos to make informed decisions.

List Your Property

Property owners can easily list their rentals with our comprehensive posting system. Add photos, descriptions, amenities, and set your pricing.

Manage with Dashboard

Use the comprehensive dashboard to manage your properties, track bookings, view analytics, and communicate with tenants all in one place.

Connect & Communicate

Connect with property owners or potential tenants through our integrated messaging system for seamless communication and coordination.
